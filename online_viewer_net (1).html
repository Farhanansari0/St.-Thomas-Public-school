About Our School

Located in Suriya, Giridih, St. Thomas Public School is more than just an educational institution; it is a community where students are encouraged to explore, innovate, and grow. Since our inception, we have strived to create an environment that blends intellectual curiosity with moral integrity.

Our curriculum is designed to meet modern standards while rootings students in traditional values, ensuring they are prepared for the challenges of tomorrow.
